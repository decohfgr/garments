DecoHF Group Textile was established in the year 2003, in Al-Hassan Industrial Estate, the world's first Qualifying Industrial Zone. It then had only two production lines engaging 300 people and 130 machines and I s turnover, then, was just 2 million US dollars per annum. Now, one and a half decades later, DecoHF Group has over 12,000 machines and 24000 employees, producing over 250,000 garments per day achieving a total output of 66.2 million garments in 2017, earning an annual turnover exceeding 458 million dollars. In the garment manufacturing sector in Jordan the Company leads the export sector with a 26.5% share of Jordan's total garment exports. DecoHF Group supplies garments of top quality such as active wear - knit tops and bottoms, outer wear - jackets, woven denim and twill bottoms, woven tops, uniform wear - jackets and bottoms, formal wear - pants and tops, work wear, etc. to world leaders in retail marketing like Wal-Mart Stores Inc., Under Armour Inc., Hanes Brand Inc., GAP Inc., Gear for Sports Inc., American Eagle Outfitters Inc., One Jeanswear Group, J.C. Penney Company Inc., Adidas AG, etc. The Company has been included in the Golden List of Companies established by the Ministry of Labor of the Kingdom of Taiwan. It is also a member of ILO-IFC Better Work Program. .

Hours of work

DecoHF Group ensures that the hours worked each day and the days worked each week do not exceed the legal maximum. One day off is allowed in every seven-day period, except as required to meet urgent business needs, in which case additional compensation is paid for the extra work.

Compensation and benefits

The minimum compensation required by local law, including mandated wages, overtime allowances, additional legally dictated benefits and payments according to the terms of their employment are paid to the workers and other employees of DecoHF group without any delay in payment.

No discrimination

DecoHF employs, pays, promotes and/or terminates workers purely on the basis of their ability and efficiency in the discharge of their duties. No discrimination is made on the basis of their personal characteristics which are irrelevant to the execution of their job or their personal beliefs. No distinction or discrimination is ever made or allowed on the basis of age, gender, nationality, political opinions, race, caste, creed, social status etc.

Strategy

Progress of production is reported to customers and if there is any unavoidable delay, extension of delivery date is requested and obtained. Arrangements are made with third party inspection firms, for final inspection by them, and plans are finalised for final shipment.
